Frequently Asked Questions

What sets Black Cat Media apart from other companies?

We stand out from the competition through our comprehensive suite of services including web design, graphic design, photography, and videography. With a focus on creativity, quality, and client satisfaction, we consistently elevate brands and exceed industry standards.

How far in advance should you book our services?

We recommend booking our services at least 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure availability and to allow ample time for thorough planning and execution of your project.

Do we require a deposit or upfront payment?

Yes, we require a 50% deposit upfront to secure your booking and initiate the project. The remaining balance is due upon project completion.

What happens if you aren't satisfied with the final product?

Client satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not satisfied with the final product, we will work closely with you to make the necessary revisions until you are happy with the outcome.
